Rest with a View of Athens: Electra Metropolis

In the historic heart of Athens, there is a place where you can experience modern relaxation and the charm of antiquity simultaneously. This is where modern design and classic aesthetics harmoniously blend. In the morning, enjoy breakfast on the rooftop bathed in sunlight, with a majestic view of the Acropolis. This stunning view will give you a refreshing start to your day.

In the evening, you can relax comfortably in your room or soothe your tired body at the hotel's luxurious spa. The interior of the hotel is elegant and cozy, making you feel like you are resting in your own private space. The high level of service and convenient facilities ensure that your stay is special.

Hidden Gem of the Coastal City: Phidias Piraeus Hotel

Set against the beautiful backdrop of the Piraeus coastline, this hotel offers travelers a unique experience. Located in a place where you can feel the charm of the ancient port city, a few steps away reveals the beach and picturesque cafes where you can enjoy an exotic morning alongside yachts. The vibrant markets and unique local restaurants around the hotel promise an exciting exploration.

Each room is stylish and modernly designed, with sunlight brightening the room through large windows. The friendly and attentive staff are always ready to help ensure your comfortable stay. At the hotel's cozy café, you can start your morning with fresh coffee, enjoying the peaceful Mediterranean atmosphere. Here, you experience not just a stay but a unique blend of culture and nature.

🔗 Phidias Piraeus Hotel
