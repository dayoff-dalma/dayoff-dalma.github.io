Top 5 Beach Resorts in Mauritius for a Perfect Stay

Hello, this is Dalma from Dalma Travel. Mauritius is a renowned vacation destination known for its turquoise waters and sun-soaked beaches. If you're looking for the perfect stay to enjoy the best of Mauritius, I recommend Constance Belle Mare Plage, C Mauritius All Inclusive, LUX Grand Gaube, LUX Le Morne, and Preskil Beach Resort. Each hotel offers stunning scenery, luxurious amenities, and beautiful beaches, captivating the hearts of travelers. No matter which hotel you choose, your trip to Mauritius will be an unforgettable experience.

Dream Beach Vacation at Constance Belle Mare Plage

Imagine a vacation in Mauritius, often called a paradise on earth. This place, where the emerald sea unfolds before your eyes and the fine sand tickles your toes, is where you can have a memorable holiday. Known for its cozy balconies overlooking the sea, Constance Belle Mare Plage offers an ideal start to your day, accompanied by various restaurants serving delicious cuisines from around the world. Additionally, it is perfect for active travelers with its wide range of water sports and golf courses.

This place, offering unforgettable experiences, is particularly loved by families. With various play facilities for children and professional kids' programs, parents can also enjoy a relaxing break. As if ending the day, the breathtaking sunset over the sea every evening is a privilege exclusive to this place.

🔗 Constance Belle Mare Plage

Maximum Relaxation, Only Here

C Mauritius - All Inclusive

A recent guest described this place as "a true paradise." Every moment at this special accommodation on Mauritius Island feels magical. Spend the entire day relaxing on the beach and end it with a successful barbecue party in the evening. The well-maintained, clean rooms and friendly staff have already been verified by many guests. Particularly, the various restaurants here receive rave reviews from visitors. Fresh seafood enjoyed while overlooking the sea is truly unforgettable.

🔗 C Mauritius - All Inclusive

A Postcard-Worthy Resort

LUX* Grand Gaube

If you want to feel like you've arrived in a tropical paradise, this beachfront resort is perfect for relaxing in a serene and clean environment. Enjoy delicious food from around the world at various restaurants while watching the charming evening sunset. Luxurious spa treatments and attractive swimming pools will completely relieve your fatigue. It provides all the romantic moments couples need.

Take a morning walk along the spacious garden or beach with a gentle breeze. If you're lucky, you might spot a group of cute dolphins, making for a picture-perfect moment against the vast ocean backdrop. Whether you're an experienced surfer or trying it for the first time, professional guides are always ready to help. All of this will make your vacation unforgettable.

🔗 LUX* Grand Gaube

Romantic Beach Scenery, a Healing Retreat

LUX* Le Morne

End your day looking at picturesque hotel scenery that seems too beautiful to be real. The serene and peaceful atmosphere deeply calms your mind, making you forget all your worries. The sparkling sunset viewed from a relaxed terrace on the beach will provide you with the special moments you've been looking for.

Discover a new you at this perfect hotel, from spa services to the attentive staff. Every moment with the friendly smiles of the staff washes away the fatigue of travel, and a night in the luxurious rooms recharges you for the next day. This perfect relaxation spot will make you want to return.

🔗 LUX* Le Morne

An Unforgettable Visit

Preskil Island Resort

Enjoy a surprising and relaxing stay at this hidden gem hotel located on the dazzling beach of Mauritius. The warm beach sun and soft sand wash away the stress of everyday life. The lush natural scenery around the hotel, combined with high-quality service and comfortable rooms, leads you to ultimate relaxation. How about a leisurely coconut water on the beautiful beach? All of this is waiting for you.

At the restaurant overlooking the clear sea, you can taste dishes made with fresh local ingredients. Just looking at the sea while eating provides a healing experience. The fantastic scenery along with delicious food will make your Mauritius trip even more special. Whether

with family, a loved one, or friends, you can spend meaningful time here. Each day here will remain an unforgettable memory.

🔗 Preskil Island Resort

Conclusion

Each of these hotels has its own unique charm. Constance Belle Mare Plage offers an open sea view and active travel options for a relaxing stay. C Mauritius - All Inclusive provides a relaxing and enjoyable stay. LUX* Grand Gaube is perfect for romantic sunsets and luxurious spa treatments. LUX* Le Morne offers perfect relaxation with peaceful nature and attentive service. Finally, Preskil Island Resort is a place for beautiful beaches and creating special memories. Choose one of these hotels to plan a special trip!
